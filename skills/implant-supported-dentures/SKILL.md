# Implant-Supported Dentures Workflow

## Overview
This is a reference guide for implant-supported denture cases using Implant Direct implants with Zest LOCATOR abutments.

---

## Step 1: Determine Implant Platform Size

**Critical First Step:** Identify the platform diameter of the implants you placed.

**Implant Direct Platform Options:**
- 3.0mm (small platform)
- 3.5mm (regular platform)
- 4.5mm (standard platform - most common)

**How to Find Your Platform Size:**
1. Check the implant surgical records/chart
2. Review the implant boxes/packaging from surgery
3. Look at the surgical guide or case plan documentation
4. Check the radiograph if implant markings are visible

---

## Step 2: Select Zest LOCATOR Abutments

Once you know your platform size, order the matching LOCATOR abutments.

### Zest LOCATOR Options for Implant Direct:

**3.0mm Platform**
- Available in cuff heights: 0.73mm, 2.0mm, 3.0mm, 4.0mm, 5.0mm, 6.0mm
- Choose based on soft tissue height

**3.5mm Platform**
- Available in cuff heights: 0mm, 1.0mm, 2.5mm, 3.5mm, 4.5mm, 5.5mm, 6.5mm
- Most flexibility for tissue contours

**4.5mm Platform (Purple)**
- Available in cuff heights: 0mm, 1.0mm, 2.0mm, 3.0mm, 4.0mm
- Most common/popular

### Cuff Height Selection:
- **Measure** the distance from implant platform to soft tissue crest
- **Choose cuff height** that positions the abutment top at or slightly above tissue level
- **Standard:** 3.0-4.0mm cuff height for most cases

---

## Step 3: RTX vs. Standard LOCATOR

**RTX vs. Standard Options:**
- **RTX:** Recommended for certain cases, appears to be a design variation
- **Standard LOCATOR:** Traditional option

*Note: Confirm with Zest which is compatible with your Implant Direct implant system*

---

## Step 4: Order from Zest

### Required Information for Order:
1. ✅ Platform size (3.0mm, 3.5mm, or 4.5mm)
2. ✅ Cuff height (based on tissue measurement)
3. ✅ Quantity: **2** (for bilateral placement)
4. ✅ Verify RTX vs. standard LOCATOR compatibility

### Order Contact:
- **Company:** Zest Dental Solutions
- **Website:** www.zestdent.com
- **Product:** LOCATOR Implant Direct Legacy Abutment (or RTX variant)

---

## Step 5: Clinical Placement

1. **Screw selection:** Use appropriate screw for abutment-implant interface
2. **Torque:** Follow manufacturer specifications (~25 Ncm typical)
3. **Seat carefully:** Ensure full seating on implant platform
4. **Verify contact:** Check denture retention test
5. **Retention adjustment:** Locators provide flexibility for retention tuning

## Common Issues & Solutions

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Denture retention too loose | Increase retention (adjust denture or use different retention caps) |
| Denture retention too tight | Decrease retention (adjust retention mechanism) |
| Uneven retention | Ensure both abutments are at same height; verify parallel placement |
| Abutment screw loosening | Check torque specification; consider screw-locking agent |
